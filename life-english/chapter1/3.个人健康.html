# Unit 1 体重过重 Overweight

TIP

Amy: Peter, whaat are you doing at the moment?

Peter: Nothing. I'm thinking about killing time in that new ice cream shop.

Amy: I don't think it is a good idea. Did you forget you're on a diet?

Peter: Oh, I forgot. I do need to lose weight for the sake of my health.

Amy: Yeah, you're indeed overweight.

# 词汇详解

  1. at the moment 现在， 此刻。 for the moment 暂时； 目前

例：Mary has nothing to do at the moment. 玛丽现在无事可做。

Everything seemed perfect for the moment. 目前一切看来似乎都很完美

  1. kill time 消磨时间

例： Listening to music is a good way to kill time. 听音乐是消磨时间的好方法。

  1. be on a diet 节食

例：Natalie is on a diet for her upcoming wedding. 娜塔莉为了她即将到来的婚礼节食。

  1. lose weight 减肥 gain weight 增肥(=put on weight) overweight adj 过重， 超重

例： Tim has lost weight due to his illness. 由于疾病， 提姆瘦下来了。

Daniel is too thin; he should gain some weight. 丹尼尔太瘦了， 他应该加加些重量。

Eating too many sweets will easily cause you to be overweight. 吃太多甜食容易导致你体重过重。

  1. for the sake of ... 为了。。。。

例： You should fasten your seat belt for the sake of personal safety. 为了个人安全， 你应该系紧安全带。

  1. indeed adv 真正地

例：Monet's works are indeed great masterpieces. 莫奈的作品的确是伟大的杰作。

  1. A healthy diet shoult be combined with regular exercise. 健康的饮食应与规律的运动相结合。

  3. It's unhealthy that Tom doesn't eat breakfast every morning. 汤姆每天早上都不吃早餐， 这样是不健康的。
  4. A good diet should include fruits, vegetables, and healthy grains. 健康的饮食应包含水果， 蔬菜和健康的谷物类食物。
combine vt 使结合， 使联合
be combined with 与。。。。结合
grain n 谷物

  1. Eating junk food is an unhealthy habit. 吃垃圾食物是个不健良的习惯
  2. Too much protein may cause health problems. 摄取过多的蛋白质容易引起健康问题。

  2. If you want to lose weight, you should avoid high calorie food. 如果你想减肥， 就应该避免高热量食物。
  3. I've put on 20 pounds in the past few months. 我过去几个月内胖了20磅。

# Unit 4 失眠 Sleeplessness

TIP

Lincoln: You seemed to be distracted at the meeting this aftermoon. Are you feeling Ok?

Daisy: I'm sorry, I'm not myself today. I feel weary, and I'm so exhausted.

Lincoln: Hmm, you don't look the best. Do you suffer from insomnia?

Daisy: Yeah, I had trouble sleeping last night because I drank too much coffee. Lincoln: In that case, you should go home and rest

# 词汇详解

  1. distracted a. 思绪不集中的 distract vt 使分心 例：The distracted player was unable to catch the ball in time to win the game. 那名不专心的球员无法及时接到球来赢得那场比赛。

  2. weary adj. 疲倦的

例： Zoey looks pale and weary because of her illness. 因为疾病， 佐伊看起来面色苍白又疲惫。

  1. exhausted adj. 疲惫的 be exhausted 累坏了= be tired out = be worn out

例： Iwas exhausted after all work. 把全部的工作完成后， 我累坏了。

  1. suffer vt 遭受， 经历 & vi. 受折磨

suffer from 饱受。。。之苦； 罹患。。。疾病

例：Scott has suffered from servere migraines since he was 15 years old. 斯科特自15岁以来一直饱受偏头痛之苦。

  • migraine n 偏头痛
  1. insomnia n 失眠 insomnia n 失眠症患者
